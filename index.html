Instructions for Game Play

Most of you are familiar withthe game BATTLESHIP, known worldwide as a pencil and paper game which dates from World War I. In 1967 Milton Bradley introduced a version of the game that used plastic boards and pegs. I have writtten a version in plain Javascript, to home my skills. I hope you enjoy it.

Summary: Each player has a 10 x 10 board, on which they hide 5 ships (Carrier - 5 holes, Battleship - 4 holes, Cruiser' - 3 holes, Submarine - 3 holes, Destroyer' - 2 holes), After hiding their ships, players take turns taking randon shots, hoping to hit their opponent's ship. First player to sink all 5 ships is the winnder!
